# Release Notes

## Complete safety-box environment

This release starts from the full original `Helicopter.zip` and preserves the complete Poisson, CBF/HOCBF, and vision packages. It adds reusable CLF, contingency, core-contract, and unified-filter packages without replacing the original libraries with reduced versions.

## New research workflows

- predefined 3-D obstacle-world experiment;
- offline single-image experiment;
- live video/camera experiment;
- HOCBF, CLF, ROA, forcing, and Poisson-solver studies;
- publication-grade PNG/PDF/SVG figures;
- raw CSV/JSON/NPZ data and effective configurations;
- target-failure and certified-diversion scenarios.
